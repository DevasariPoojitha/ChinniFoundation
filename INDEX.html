About Us

Chinnifoundation is a charitable social initiative dedicated to building a hunger-free and compassionate society. Our foundation focuses on donating food to roadside and homeless people, supporting basic educational needs, spreading social awareness, and helping orphans, physically challenged individuals, and elderly people without family support. We believe that no human being should suffer from hunger, loneliness, or lack of basic necessities. Through regular food distribution drives, education support programs, and care initiatives, Chinnifoundation1 works to uplift the most vulnerable sections of society, including orphaned children and senior citizens living in orphanages. Our efforts are driven by humanity, kindness, and responsibility. By serving those in need with dignity and respect, we aim to bring hope, awareness, and positive change to communities. Our Mission To eliminate hunger, support basic education, care for orphans and the elderly, and promote social awareness through selfless service. Our Vision A society where no one goes hungry, every child has access to education, and the vulnerable live with dignity and care.
